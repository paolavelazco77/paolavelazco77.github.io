Paola Velazco is an American born Mexican Web Developer. She speaks English, Italian and Spanish fluently. Velazco grew up in Tijuana. She studied Communications at La Universidad Iberoamerica in Mexico City.

At the beggining of her career she collaborated as a writer in a couple of magazines like Vice, Noisey, Nylon, Picnic, ERRR, Playboy and many others. In 2015 she moved to New York City to study Digital Editing.

Since then she has been working in projects as an video editor like Documentary Films, Feauture Films, Commercials spots. Currently she lives in Brooklyn, New York.
